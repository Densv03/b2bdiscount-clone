What is Sourcing Request?
Sourcing Request is a tool that assists traders with vendor sourcing and helps vendors to find new outlets for their goods. On Sourcing Request, buyers request what they want and get quotes from direct suppliers.
How does it work?
For buyers
Сreate a Request, indicating your precise requirements for the goods and their price. Vendors worldwide will see your request and send their quotations.
For vendors
Browse the Sourcing Request section to find requests for the product you produce or sell. Use filters to sort Requests by category or country. Press the “Quote Now” button to make an offer.

What is Unclaimed cargo?
Unclaimed cargo is regular cargo that the consignee has rejected. It’s not damaged, broken, or stale. It’s just not wanted anymore. The shipper of this cargo is legally allowed to resell the goods to a new buyer.
How does it work?
Register on the platform
Sign up for free to get access to the Unclaimed Cargo section.
Upload the unclaimed cargo
Add your offer, including a cargo description, docs and photos.
Wait for messages from buyers
Potential buyers will contact you through the built-in chat.

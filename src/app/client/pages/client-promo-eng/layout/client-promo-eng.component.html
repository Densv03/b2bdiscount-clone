A proactive approach

The purpose of the platform is not just to help exporters and traders with unclaimed cargo but also to prevent the emergence of such goods.

If you have doubts about the reliability of the consignee, take action now. Put your cargo on Globy to find other potential buyers. They can be your backup plan if the initial deal doesn't work out as expected.
